Book of 2018

Posted on: 2018-12-19

Here is the list of book I read in 2018. You can consult my list of book I read in 2017 in my 2017's accomplishments.

  • Talk Like TED: The 9 Public-Speaking Secrets of the World's Top Minds
  • WAKE UP!: Escaping Life on Autopilot
  • How successful people think by John C. Maxwell
  • The autobiography of Andrew Carnegie by Andrew Carnegie
  • Jump: Take the Leap of Faith to Achieve Your Life of Abundance by Steve Harvey
  • The Power Of Your Subconscious Mind by Joseph Murphy
  • The Instant Millionaire: A Tale of Wisdom and Wealth by Mark Fisher
  • To overcome fear and worry by Joseph Murphy
  • The Willpower Instinct: How Self-Control Works, Why It Matters, and What You Can Do to Get More of It
  • The 21 Irrefutable Laws of Leadership
  • The Talent Code - Unlocking the secret of skill in sport, art, music, math and just about anything.
  • The Hacker Playbook 2: Practical Guide To Penetration Testing
  • Fundamentals of Database Systems (7th Edition)
  • Life Changing Secrets from the 3 Masters of Success: Three Habits to Achieve Abundance in Your Finances, Your Relationships, Your Health, and Your Life
  • What Hedge Funds Really Do: An Introduction to Portfolio Management
  • Machine Learning by Tom M. Mitchell
  • The Noticer
  • The Big Leap: Conquer your hidden fear and take your life to the next level
  • What hedge funds really do
  • Principles Life and Work (Ray Dalio)
  • Python for Finance: Analyze big financial data
  • Born to win, Find your success by Zig Ziglar
  • Unfu*k yourself by Gary John Bishop
  • Self-Esteem by Matthew McKay
  • Rome: An empire's story by Greg Woolf

Next year, I plan to write down a paragraph for each book but for this year, it will be a plain list.
