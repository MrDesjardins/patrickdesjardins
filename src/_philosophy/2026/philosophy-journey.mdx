I have always been captivated by the deep questions of life. My mind has a tendency to wander, at times, I even find myself getting lost in the sheer scale of my own thoughts.

Up until now, my education, hobbies, and major life choices have largely been driven by the need to improve my position in a capitalist society. I am proud of what I have achieved so far, especially in my technical career. However, as I grow older, I realize the importance of expanding my horizons and pursuing what truly matters to me before it’s too late.

With the rise of AI and Large Language Models, the intersection between Philosophy and Computer Science has never been more vital or more fascinating. To explore this, I am pursuing a degree path in Philosophy to deepen my understanding of logic and formal systems. I am officially a Candidate for Philosophy (Logic and Epistemology Concentration)!

I have dedicated a new section of this website to a Philosophy blog. Here, I will be sharing fables, essays, and various reflections as I navigate this new intellectual journey. I look forward to sharing these learnings with you.
